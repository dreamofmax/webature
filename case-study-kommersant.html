Kommersant

Social Media Marketing Paid Advertising Content Strategy

Kommersant is one of Russia's oldest and most respected business and news publications, with a legacy spanning over three decades. Known for its authoritative financial and political reporting, the brand needed to establish a dominant presence on Telegram — the platform that had become the primary news channel for Russian-speaking audiences globally.

They came to us to grow their subscriber base aggressively, without compromising editorial credibility.

The Challenge

A trusted name that needed to compete in a crowded feed

Telegram's news ecosystem is brutally competitive. Thousands of channels fight for attention daily, and audience loyalty is fragile — one slow news cycle and readers move on. Kommersant had strong brand equity offline and on the web, but their Telegram presence hadn't kept pace.

At 65,000 subscribers, the channel was punching well below its weight relative to the brand's reach. Growth had plateaued, organic discovery was limited, and the content cadence wasn't optimised for the platform's consumption habits.

The goal: reach 200,000+ subscribers within the year, while keeping the tone and standards the brand is known for.

The Approach

Content precision combined with targeted paid growth

We built a dual-track strategy: sharpen the organic content to retain and convert, then amplify with paid advertising to accelerate discovery.

  • Content audit and posting cadence restructure — identifying peak engagement windows and aligning output to them
  • Editorial format development — shorter, sharper posts designed for Telegram's mobile-first read behaviour
  • Headline and hook optimisation — testing different opening lines to improve message preview click-through
  • Paid subscriber acquisition campaigns across Telegram Ads targeting Russian-speaking news audiences globally
  • Cross-promotion strategy with aligned channels to build credibility and reach
  • Monthly performance reporting with iterative creative and targeting adjustments
The Results

From 65,000 to 240,000 — a 266% increase in under a year

The channel grew from 65,000 to 240,000 subscribers over the course of the campaign — a 266% increase that placed Kommersant's Telegram firmly among the top-tier business news channels on the platform.

Engagement rates improved alongside subscriber numbers, validating the content strategy work. The audience that came in through paid acquisition stayed and read — not just subscribed and left.

Kommersant now has a Telegram presence that matches the weight of their wider editorial brand.
