Sterling Pines
Golf Club

Web Design Web Development SEO

Sterling Pines Golf Club is a private members' golf club focused on delivering a premium on-course experience. Despite a strong reputation locally, their digital presence didn't reflect the quality of what they offered — an outdated website that was difficult to navigate on mobile and all but invisible in local search.

We came in to do it properly: new website, built from scratch, with SEO woven in from the first line of code.

The Challenge

A premium club with a website that didn't show it

Golf clubs operate in a high-consideration category. Prospective members research thoroughly before making contact, and a website that loads slowly, looks dated on mobile, or can't be found in Google is often the end of the conversation before it starts.

Sterling Pines had a loyal existing membership but struggled to attract new members through digital channels. Their old site wasn't indexed correctly for local search terms, had no clear conversion path for membership enquiries, and wasn't usable on mobile — where the majority of their traffic was landing.

The brief was clear: build something that matches the club's standing and works hard for them in search.

The Approach

Design and SEO built together, not bolted on after

We ran design and technical SEO in parallel from day one — not as separate phases. Structure, page naming, content hierarchy, and metadata were all planned before a single layout was finalised.

  • Full discovery phase — understanding the member journey, key conversion goals, and local search landscape
  • Site architecture planned around SEO intent: membership, green fees, course information, and contact
  • Mobile-first design throughout — every layout built and tested on small screens first
  • Clean, fast codebase with performance-optimised images and minimal dependencies
  • On-page SEO implementation: title tags, meta descriptions, structured data, internal linking
  • Google Business Profile alignment to strengthen local search presence
  • Clear membership enquiry flow with reduced friction from landing to contact
The Results

A site that works as hard off the tee as on it

Sterling Pines launched with a website that reflects the quality of the club — clean, fast, and built to be found. The membership-focused structure gives prospective members everything they need to make a decision, and a clear path to get in touch when they're ready.

The SEO foundations are in place from day one: correctly structured pages, optimised metadata, local schema, and a site fast enough to rank well on Core Web Vitals. Organic visibility is already improving month on month since launch.

The club now has a digital presence that matches what they've always delivered on the course.
