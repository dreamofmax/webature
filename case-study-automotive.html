An established automotive brand wanted to drive mass awareness and genuine cultural conversation around a new model launch. Traditional media alone wasn't enough — they needed to meet their audience where they actually spend time, and in a voice they actually trust.

The Challenge

Cutting through in a category where everyone is shouting

The automotive space is one of the most contested categories in influencer marketing. Every brand is in creators' feeds, budgets are large, and audiences are increasingly sceptical of paid partnerships that feel transactional.

The brand needed a campaign that didn't just generate impressions — it needed to generate conviction. The brief called for authentic content that would reach car enthusiasts, lifestyle audiences, and younger first-time buyers simultaneously, across three very different platform cultures.

The challenge was coordination: getting the right message delivered in the right format, by the right voices, on the right platform — without it feeling like a corporate rollout.

The Approach

A tiered creator mix built for reach and resonance

We structured the campaign around three creator tiers, each with a distinct role in the funnel:

TierSizeRole
Nano / Micro5k – 50kAuthenticity and community trust — high engagement, niche audiences
Mid-tier50k – 500kBroad reach with retained credibility — the backbone of the campaign
Macro / Mega500k – 1m+Mass awareness and cultural moment — launch amplification
  • Platform-specific creative briefs — short-form vertical for TikTok & Reels, long-form review content for YouTube
  • Creator vetting and outreach — selecting for audience alignment, not just follower count
  • Content review and approval workflow to maintain brand standards without stifling creator voice
  • Campaign tracking across all three platforms with unified reporting
  • Performance optimisation mid-campaign — doubling down on best-performing creators and formats
The Results

Multi-platform reach with measurable impact across all tiers

The campaign delivered across all three objectives: reach, engagement, and sentiment. The tiered creator approach proved its value — micro creators drove exceptional engagement rates among niche enthusiasts, while macro creators produced the cultural moment the launch needed.

Platform-specific content outperformed generic brand content across every metric measured. TikTok drove the highest volume of organic reshares; YouTube reviews generated sustained long-tail views weeks after the launch window.

Specific metrics are withheld under NDA, but the campaign has since informed the brand's ongoing influencer strategy.
